Reflektor

Reflektor is a bouncing puzzle game made with Unity. The goal of the game is to use a projectile to hit a target on the other side of level. To do so, players will need to bounce their projectiles off the level's walls and obstacles. Be careful, though: each projectile can bounce only a few times before being destroyed, and your supply is limited.
