About

Rowan Lovejoy - Software and Video Game Developer

Computers have been a part of my life and source of great joy - and frustration - for as long as I can remember. A career working with them seemed natural then. After working my way through a couple of computing and software development related BTECs and a foundation degree, I am now taking an undergraduate computing & games development course at University of Plymouth.

Video games are one of my greatest passions. They have helped me to grow as person and make connections with others. And so, I want to give back to them by developing my own games that champion strongly focused gameplay and player-friendly design.

My portfolio is small, but growing rapidly, along with my skills. Eventually, I hope to earn a living from my work and contribute to larger projects.

My projects are accessible from the Portfolio page. Please see the Contact page if you wish to get in touch.
