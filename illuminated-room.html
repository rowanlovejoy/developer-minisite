Illuminated Room

3D interactive environment with first-person navigation, created using 3ds Max and Unity

Illuminated Room is an interactive 3D environment. This project was created as part of university coursework. A unique limitation was that the design of the environment should be based on information obtained from interviewing a fellow student about their ideal room. My interview desired an attic room with a sloped roof and skylight, and so I created the Illuminated Room environment to meet these requirements.

This project required me to produce a large quantity of 3D assets, and so gave me considerable practice using 3Ds Max, including modelling and unwrapping, and setting up polygon groups to enable multiple material slots in Unity. The main interactive feature of the environment is the ability to turn the lights on and off, presenting the player with two different views of the room. Implementing this feature was more challenging than expect due to encountering issues with Unity’s lighting system, such as light bleeding around objects, but I was ultimately able to achieve an effect I was happy with, with some compromises.
